What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios, where players can explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world. Players can mine resources, build structures, craft items, and fight enemies. The game has various modes, including Creative, Survival, and Adventure, and is available on multiple platforms such as PC, consoles, and mobile devices.
